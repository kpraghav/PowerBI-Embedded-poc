Tablix Editor (first Tablix only)

This editor maps the first <Tablix> found in the RDL to an editable HTML table. It supports add/remove/rename header columns and will update the corresponding <TablixColumns>, header cells and detail cells where simple Fields!FieldName.Value patterns are used.
